Numeric Keypad
The location of each key on the numeric keypad corresponds to a display area.
Key Description
1 Display the lower left of the area.
2 Display the lower center of the area.
3 Display the lower right of the area.
4 Display the middle left of the area.
5 Display the middle center of the area.
6 Display the middle right of the area.
7 Display the upper left of the area.
8 Display the upper center of the area.
9 Display the upper right of the area.
